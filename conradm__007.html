Ahoj!

My name is Conrad M. Meissner. I am a poet and self-declared generalist. Currently, I am working at SAP to make a living. In the long run I want to work with literature and theatre to cultivate empathy among and with ourselves.

I consider myself to be an engaged citizen of Europe and its democratic future. Beyond that, I am passionate about chocolate, manga and the yennoistic project.

Below you can find a comprised timeline of some of the more decisive milestones that have shaped my life not eschewing failures as well as including moments of which I am proud to have been a part.

You're welcome to get in touch!

Unsuccessful Interview at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Germany (Höherer Dienst).

I follow my girlfriend to live in Prague, CZ.

Graduation Speech before the 2022 / 23 EMGS Cohort.

Acceptance of my Master Thesis "Emanzipatorische Poesie by the University of Vienna.

Publication of "Mauern" in Hebrew translation.

Coming home from afar, my treasured coffee mug breaks in my back. It was orange.

The very first edition of "Apropos Lesebühne". One of many.

A friend shows me Carlos Drummond's "No Meio do Caminho". I think it's silly.

Debut Performance of "Das Interview" ("The Interview").

My friends and I found the band "The Bacon Stripes". Germany has just won the World Cup.

I board a plane to Scottsburg, IN wearing a grey jacket which fits too tightly over my belly.

"Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows" is released. My brother and I finish it within a week.

My parents and both my grand-mothers take my brother and me to the seaside where we learn how to sail.

My brother and I start writing a book. We call it "Der Phantasie Planet" ("The Phantasy Planet").

I learn how to speak by talking to my twin brother.

My mother gives birth to me in Austin, TX. I'm very grateful, not having done much myself up until that point.
